Instructions

Nodes

Add a node:

Drag a node from the toolbar into the canvas

Remove a node:

Select a node with Left Mouse and hit the Delete-Key on your keyboard

Label a node:

Select a node with Left Mouse and type a name on your keyboard

Organize the nodes:

Click the organize button on the bottom of the page

Edges

Add an edge:

Click onto the line in the toolbar and an edge will be created between your last two selections (as indicated by the green color)
